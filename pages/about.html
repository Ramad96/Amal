About Amal

Hope has a home.

Amal (أمل) means hope in Arabic. It's the feeling behind every dua — the trust that Allah hears, that He responds, that nothing is lost.

This app is a simple, quiet place to keep your duas close. Save the ones that move you. Write your own. Share them when you're blessed to be somewhere holy. Let others say Ameen.

Made by Amanah Digital

An Islamic digital studio dedicated to building tools that nurture iman and serve the Ummah.
